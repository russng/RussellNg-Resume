Russell Ng

Pre-Business

Russell is currently a freshman enrolled at the University of California, Riverside for Pre-Business, and is currently looking into Business Administration with a concentration in Information Systems.

Russell graduated from Terra Nova High School in Pacifica, California in May 2019, and has worked and volunteered in many capacities. Russell has worked with children in multiple disciplines, including tutoring and teaching in a religious context.

His passion for work cannot be understated - Russell was one of the top in my high school class with a 4.08 GPA, in addition to leading in the school's band for three years and participating in a multitude of other extra-curriculars, including Students In Action and Badminton. Additionally, Russell has been consistently volunteering at his church for many years in their Summer programs, even being in charge of the camp for two years. Furthermore, Russell has participated in overseas missionary work, teaching children in Thailand about Christianity, while also helping to build a clean water system in a village in Northern Thailand. He hopes to graduate from UCR in 2023, and continue to collaborate with others.
